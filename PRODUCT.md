# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Indian manufacturing enterprise suppliers (managing registrations, bids, POs, dispatch, and invoices) and internal teams (procurement, warehouse GRN, finance accounts payable) operating in fast-paced industrial environments.

## Product Purpose
VendorConnect Portal is an SAP-integrated supplier self-service platform designed to streamline and digitize the Procure-to-Pay (P2P) lifecycle. It provides real-time transparency of SAP ERP data and documents, eliminating manual paper/email workflows and offering full visibility into GST/TDS transaction payload flows.

## Brand Personality
Industrial-strength, Trustworthy, Seamless. The voice is professional and reassuringly clear, balancing enterprise-grade authority with modern usability.

## Anti-references
Cluttered legacy SAP GUI screens (transaction codes XK01/ME21N/MIRO), low-contrast "AI-style" light gray on off-white dashboards, generic minimalist SaaS tools that fail to handle dense industrial tables.

## Design Principles
1. **Payload Transparency**: Expose simulated BAPI/RFC XML/JSON payloads directly to demystify integration status.
2. **Refined Grid Density**: Adapt structured SAP Fiori density guidelines into a clean, modern React SPA interface without wasting whitespace.
3. **Uncompromising Contrast**: Build high-visibility interfaces for factory floor screens and varied lighting conditions.
4. **India-Specific Compliance First**: Explicitly highlight GST, TDS, MSME compliance status as primary visual anchors.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with strict color contrast (≥4.5:1 for all readable text), keyboard-navigable forms for high-speed data entry, and respect for prefers-reduced-motion queries.
